Our Delivery Model

Mitra US Provides Two Delivery Models. Choice of Delivery Model is dictated by client.

  • Onshore - Delivery Model
  • Onshore/Offshore - Delivery Model
  • US based team
  • Available to work on projects either individually or as a team
  • Available onsite or remotely


Mitra US is a full-service application development company that saves time and money for its clients with an Onshore-Offshore Business Model.
The proven model combines an onshore developer with two to three offshore developers.
The onshore developer is available during US business hours for tasks that need to be addressed quickly. This developer is available for scheduled meetings, gather requirement and review code from offshore team.
The review process involves checking code quality, coding standards and making sure deliverables meet client requirements.
